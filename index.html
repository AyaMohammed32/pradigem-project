About Paradigm Company

PSdigital has started in March 2007 as a web development company. Our mission was providing all kinds of websites as needed by potential clients, varying from basic web presence to complex service oriented websites. The market has changed a lot since then, and the perceived value of digital marketing power began to rise. We have succeeded to evolve along with the market from a website provider, to a complete provider of digital marketing solutions.
